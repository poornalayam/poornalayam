Bhagavad Gita – Chapter 16 (பகவத்கீதை – அத்யாயம் 16)

This chapter is called Daivasurasampadvibhaga-yoga since it deals with daiva (spiritual) and asura (materialistic) traits that are to be followed and to be avoided respectively, by a seeker.

The summary of Bhagavad Gita – Chapter 16, given by Swami Paramarthananda, is available for download.

Visit the Bhagavad Gita – Chapter 16 page on The Internet Archive.
